The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in English and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized version were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained England estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with soome fruits trees.
